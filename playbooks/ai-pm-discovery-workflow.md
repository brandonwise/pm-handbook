# AI PM Discovery Workflow

1. Collect raw signal from users, support, and telemetry
2. Cluster signal by recurring pain, not by feature request
3. Define success metric before debating solutions
4. Prototype quickly (doc or lightweight build)
5. Test with real users before scaling scope

## Guardrails
- No feature enters roadmap without a measurable outcome
- No PRD ships without explicit out-of-scope section
- No launch without rollback criteria
