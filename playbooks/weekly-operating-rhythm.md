# Weekly Operating Rhythm (AI PM)

## Monday — Direction
- Reconfirm the outcome metric
- Review last week's misses
- Align top 3 priorities with engineering lead

## Tuesday — Discovery
- 3 customer signals (calls, tickets, usage)
- Document one insight that changes scope or priority

## Wednesday — Spec quality
- Tighten PRDs and acceptance criteria
- Kill one vague requirement

## Thursday — Execution review
- Risk sweep with eng/design
- Clear blockers and make one hard tradeoff call

## Friday — Story + signal
- Ship stakeholder update
- Record wins, misses, and next-week focus
