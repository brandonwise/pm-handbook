# Sanitized Case Study: Platform Rollout Under Reliability Pressure

- Status: Example
- Scope: Sanitized for public portfolio use

## Context
A platform team was shipping quickly, but customer-facing reliability dipped after each release.

## Problem
Escalations were climbing and confidence in release quality was dropping.

## Approach
- Introduced release gates tied to quality metrics
- Shifted roadmap language from output to outcomes
- Added weekly risk review with engineering and support

## Outcome
- Escalations down 31% in six weeks
- Mean time to resolution down 22%
- Stakeholder trust improved because tradeoffs were explicit

## Lessons
- Reliability work needs a visible owner
- Vague requirements create expensive downstream ambiguity
- Weekly rhythm beats heroics
